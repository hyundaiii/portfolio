Beauty Odyssey

Beauty Odyssey is a personalized beauty app that recommends makeup tutorial videos and product suggestions based on facial analysis or manual input. It guides users to explore the best YouTube content and Sephora tools tailored to their face shape and skin type.

Frontend: React Native (Expo), Tailwind CSS
Backend: Prisma, MySQL
APIs: YouTube API, Sephora product integration (manual redirect)
